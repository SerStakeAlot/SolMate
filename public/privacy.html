Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2026

1. Introduction

SolMate ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our decentralized chess application on the Solana blockchain.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Wallet Address: Your public Solana wallet address when you connect to play
  • Game Data: Chess moves, game results, and timestamps
  • Transaction Data: Records of stakes and payouts (publicly visible on blockchain)

We do NOT collect: names, emails, passwords, IP addresses, or personal identification information.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Facilitate chess matches and process stakes
  • Display game history and statistics
  • Improve our services and user experience

4. Blockchain Data

SolMate operates on the Solana blockchain. All transactions are:

  • Publicly visible on the blockchain
  • Immutable and cannot be deleted
  • Associated with your wallet address (pseudonymous)

We have no control over blockchain data once transactions are confirmed.

5. Third-Party Services

We integrate with:

  • Solana Blockchain: For transaction processing
  • Wallet Providers: Phantom, Solflare, and other Solana wallets

These services have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.

6. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Secure WebSocket connections for real-time gameplay
  • No storage of private keys or sensitive wallet data
  • Smart contract security audits

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disconnect your wallet at any time
  • Request information about data we hold
  • Note: Blockchain transactions cannot be deleted due to the immutable nature of the technology

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact us at:

Email: privacy@playsolmate.fun

Website: https://playsolmate.fun

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated revision date.
