Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2026

1. Introduction

SolMate is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our decentralized chess application on the Solana blockchain.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Wallet Address: Your public Solana wallet address when you connect to play
  • Game Data: Chess moves, game results, and timestamps
  • Transaction Data: Records of stakes and payouts (publicly visible on blockchain)

We do NOT collect: names, emails, passwords, IP addresses, or personal identification information.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Facilitate chess matches and process stakes
  • Display game history and statistics
  • Improve our services and user experience

4. Blockchain Data

SolMate operates on the Solana blockchain. All transactions are:

  • Publicly visible on the blockchain
  • Immutable and cannot be deleted
  • Associated with your wallet address (pseudonymous)

5. Third-Party Services

We integrate with Solana Blockchain for transaction processing and wallet providers like Phantom and Solflare. These services have their own privacy policies.

6. Data Security

We implement secure WebSocket connections for real-time gameplay, no storage of private keys, and smart contract security audits.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to disconnect your wallet at any time and request information about data we hold. Note: Blockchain transactions cannot be deleted.
